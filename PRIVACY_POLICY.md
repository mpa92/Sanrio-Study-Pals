# Privacy Policy for Sanrio Study Pals

**Last Updated**: December 2025

## Overview

Sanrio Study Pals is a Chrome extension designed to help users study productively with adorable Sanrio character companions. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### Local Storage Only

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's Storage API:
- Study session history
- Friendship progress with characters
- Achievements unlocked
- Settings and preferences
- FP (Friendship Points) balance

This data never leaves your computer and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Permissions Used

- **storage**: Save your progress locally on your device
- **notifications**: Show study reminders and milestone notifications
- **alarms**: Keep the timer running in the background
- **idle**: Detect when your computer is idle (for potential future features)

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:
1. Going to Settings in the extension
2. Clicking "Reset All Data"
3. Or uninstalling the extension (which removes all local data)

## Children's Privacy

This extension is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the version number and notify users through the extension update system.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the extension's support channels.
